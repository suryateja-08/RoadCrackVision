About RoadCrackVision

Transforming road maintenance using deep learning.

Who We Are

RoadCrackVision is an Deep Learning Based platform built to modernize road infrastructure monitoring. Our system leverages advanced deep learning models to detect potholes and road damage from images, enabling faster maintenance decisions and improved public safety.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in AI-driven infrastructure monitoring, creating safer, smoother, and smarter transportation systems. We envision cities empowered by intelligent automation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize road inspection using computer vision and deep learning. By providing accurate, real-time pothole detection, we help authorities optimize maintenance efforts and reduce accidents.

Our Technology

Powered by advanced convolutional neural networks trained on real-world road datasets.

CNN Architecture

Extracts critical road surface features such as cracks, edges, and texture patterns to identify damage accurately.

MobileNet

Lightweight and optimized for fast inference, enabling real-time scanning even on limited hardware.

InceptionResNet

Combines deep residual learning with multi-scale feature extraction for enhanced detection accuracy.
